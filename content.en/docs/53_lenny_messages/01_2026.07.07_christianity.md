# The Time Abram lived in Ur

## The society
1) Geography / Agriculture: Mesopotamia encompasses the land between the Euphrates and Tigris rivers. Its environment was low annual precipitation, increased temperatures, and limited water availability.  The geography of southern Mesopotamia is such that agriculture is possible only with irrigation and with good drainage.  With irrigation, the food supply in Mesopotamia was comparable to that of the Canadian prairies.
