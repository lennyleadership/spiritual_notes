上帝创世以后的安息：  
上帝<b>做完了</b>祂的工，第七日祂就安息了。

创世记 2:1 和合本  
1 天地万物都造齐了。  
Genesis 2:1 NKJV  
1 Thus the heavens and the earth, and all the host of them, were <b>finished</b>.


创世记 2:2-3 和合本   
2 到第七日，　神造物的工已经完毕，就在第七日歇了他一切的工，安息了。  
3 神赐福给第七日，定为圣日；因为在这日，　神歇了他一切创造的工，就安息了。  

Genesis 2:2-3 NKJV  
2 And on the seventh day God ended His work which He had done, and He rested on the seventh day from all His work which He had done.  
3 Then God blessed the seventh day and sanctified it, because in it He rested from all His work which God had created and made.


上帝拯救以后的安息：  
约翰福音 19:30 和合本    
30 耶稣尝（原文作“受”）了那醋，就说：“成了！”便低下头，将灵魂交付　神了。  
John 19:30 NKJV  
30 So when Jesus had received the sour wine, He said, “It is finished!” And bowing His head, He gave up His spirit.

罗马书 8:32 和合本  
32 神既不爱惜自己的儿子，为我们众人舍了，岂不也把万物和他一同白白地赐给我们吗？  
Romans 8:32 NKJV  
32 He who did not spare His own Son, but delivered Him up for us all, how shall He not with Him also freely give us all things?


歌罗西书 2:9-10 和合本  
9 因为　神本性一切的丰盛都有形有体地居住在基督里面，  
10 你们在他里面也得了丰盛。他是各样执政掌权者的元首。  

Colossians 2:9-10 NKJV  
9 For in Him dwells all the fullness of the Godhead bodily;  
10 and you are complete in Him, who is the head of all principality and power.



人的安息：  

希伯来书 4:10 和合本  
10 因为那进入安息的，乃是歇了自己的工，正如　神歇了他的工一样。  
Hebrews 4:10 NKJV  
10 For he who has entered His rest has himself also ceased from his works as God did from His.
